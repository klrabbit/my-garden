I spent Saturday morning at the [Tipsy Bean ]([)](https://www.tipsybean.cafe/)a local coffee shop, drinking a cold brew and listening to the [Pen Addict](https://www.relay.fm/penaddict). It is actually cold, only 41º, cold brew was probably not the best choice.  Spent a fun time at the [Erie Beer Festival](https://www.wqln.org/erie-craft-beer-fest) in the afternoon. Discovered I like Red Ales. The most surprising like was the Peach Ale from Koeheler. Found the food at the Brewerie Restaurant next door to be disappointing. The hamburger was dry.
